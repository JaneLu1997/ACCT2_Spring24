For this project, I made a head jewelry that could do body modification and change human genome to mix with animal microrganisms.

I drew a sketch and started modeling in Blender. After some research, it turns out I might need to get into sculpting.

After that, I start to think about the surroundings. As this head piece is for chosen new born SimChild, it should be in a hospital setting. Tablet displaying the baby's information should also be included.
